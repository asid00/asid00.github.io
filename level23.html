Period of HOZ

Yet more esoteric was the later use of alphanumeric squares, possibly related to those of Enochian magic. “The negative space of the cross is a system of four elements”, Bauer cryptically notes in response to the journalist's inquiry into whether her practices had not drifted off into distinctly pagan territory. Individual glyphs upon these diagrams are systematically knit together by what she would call “the disintegrated body of Leviathan”, mapping connections between otherwise incompatible domains of realiy. Some of these — like the “Gate of the Spider-Princess Gngijkju” — were of particular interest to Bauer who understood them to be “subterranean tunnels between equinoctic periods”. To her, it was by way of these faults in time that there were lines of communication between the presently terminating age of Pisces and the coming age of Aquarius roundabout 150 years in the future.

Much of Bauer's writing during this time is of interest to certain ufologists as it is said to feature distinct themes of alien abduction, though the abess never expressed interest on the matter and the content remains ambiguous enough to feasibly only concern a violation of structural integrity as such:
